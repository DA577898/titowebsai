High Altitude Balloon

About the Project

Our high-altitude balloon project is aimed at collecting environmental data from the edge of space. Using a combination of cutting-edge sensors and robust engineering, the project will help us study atmospheric changes, temperature, pressure, and other factors at high altitudes.
